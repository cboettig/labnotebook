Large, Simulated Trees

Interested in comparing these power curves to much larger trees.  Note already above that the 14 taxa geospiza tree never gets above a power of 0.80, meaning that even in the most dramatic example of no phylogenetic signal will not be detected 20% of the time.  This asymmetric comparison isn’t strictly necessary, we could ask about the power in the reverse test.
