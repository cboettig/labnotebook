Abstract

Part of my dissertation research involved the detection and statistical verification of regime shifts in macroevolutionary history using phylogenetic comparative methods. The statistical work appears in the [pmc]() repository, though it is often applied in my generalization of the regime shift models to the case of a "release of constraint", which is found in the [wrightscape]() repository.
