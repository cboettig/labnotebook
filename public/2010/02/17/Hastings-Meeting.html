Reasons Warning Signals fail

  1. Present (saddle-node model) but insufficient sensitivity to detect (lack ensembles, lack sampling frequency)
  2. dissipative but warning signals are backwards (other higher dimensional bifurcations, limit cycles –> no potential)
  3. Hamiltonian instead of dissipative (centers, stochastically become random walks)
